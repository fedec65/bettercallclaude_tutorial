## Best Practices for CLAUDE.md

### What to Include
- ✅ Matter summary and key facts
- ✅ Parties (anonymize if highly sensitive)
- ✅ Legal issues and strategy
- ✅ Key documents and where to find them
- ✅ Status and next steps
- ✅ Deadlines

### What NOT to Include
- ❌ Client contact details (use matter codes)
- ❌ Privileged communications verbatim
- ❌ Settlement negotiations specifics
- ❌ Highly sensitive details that aren't needed for context

### Update Frequency
- **After client meetings**: Decisions, instructions
- **After major research**: Key findings
- **When strategy changes**: New direction
- **Before handoffs**: Current status
- **Weekly minimum**: For active matters

### Naming Convention
```
CLAUDE.md          - Current, active file
CLAUDE_archive.md  - When matter closes
```
